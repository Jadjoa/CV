Favourite Books From Danielle Steel

Danielle Steel is a bestselling American author known for her prolific output of romance and drama novels. With over 190 books to her name, she is one of the world’s best-selling authors, having sold more than 800 million copies globally. Her books often center on themes like:

  • • Love and Heartbreak
  • • Family dynamics
  • • Resilience in the face of adversity
  • • Glamorous yet troubled lives
  • • Strong female protagonists
